Artifact Name: Calibration Error
Modality: ASL MRI
Category: Quantification

Short Description:
Incorrect calibration leads to inaccurate perfusion quantification.

How It Appears in Image:
Perfusion maps show globally incorrect cerebral blood flow values.

Primary Cause:
Improper reference scan acquisition or scaling factor error.

Clinical Impact:
Misleading quantitative interpretation.

Severity Level:
Severe

Common Fix:
Verify calibration scans and ensure correct scaling during processing.
