Artifact Name: Aliasing Artifact
Modality: ASL MRI
Category: Acquisition

Short Description:
Aliasing occurs when the field of view is smaller than the imaged anatomy.

How It Appears in Image:
Parts of the brain appear wrapped around to the opposite side of the image.

Primary Cause:
Insufficient field of view during acquisition.

Clinical Impact:
Misinterpretation of anatomical structures.

Severity Level:
Moderate

Common Fix:
Increase field of view and adjust phase encoding direction.
